Nathalie Tamer is an Egyptian film director based in Cairo. A storyteller at heart, she graduated from Misr International University's Faculty of Mass Communication in 2023 — Radio & TV major.

From her first steps as an AD Trainee to winning a competitive production grant and directing her own short film, her trajectory is defined by passion, precision, and an unrelenting love for the craft of cinema.
